Send Us a Note About Your Gift

The San Gorgonio Pass Historical Society is an all-volunteer nonprofit. All donations support our archiving, education, and community history programs.

Your Impact

Every dollar preserves a story.

Donations fund archive materials, exhibit supplies, event costs, and educational outreach — keeping pass-area history accessible to everyone, free of charge.

  • $10 Annual membership — keeps you connected to every event and meeting
  • $25 Covers printing and materials for one community history presentation
  • $50 Supports our presence at a local festival or parade exhibit
  • $100 Patron-level — helps fund archival supplies and document preservation for the year

Founded 1989

All-volunteer, all community

The Society is entirely volunteer-run. There are no paid staff — your donation goes directly to preserving and sharing the history of Banning, Beaumont, Cabazon, Cherry Valley, and San Timoteo Canyon.
